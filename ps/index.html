Besta.domains

Started out as a simple evening side project, but grew to a rather ambitious Natural Language Processing AI product. After over a year of effort, it's now much better than any other domain suggestions tool. Currently finishing up the API / SaaS product for registrars, and sorting out available/aftermarket affiliate options for consumers.  Try the app: besta.domains.  Demo the API at: rapidapi.com

NLPThesaurus.com

Natural Language Processing - is a branch of AI that deals with interpreting and generating text. I got carried away building besta.domains, and accidentally built the most powerful English language thesaurus. It's the most accurate source for root-words, parts-of-speech, sentiment, and meta data like abbreviations and contractions. It also has pretty good synonyms, organized by sentiment - so you can choose to utilize only the most positive words - not to offend anybody and not to generate awkward phrases.  Will soon be available as an API. Looking for contributors and partners to expand on its potential! nlpthesaurus.com
